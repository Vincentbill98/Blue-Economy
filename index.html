Empowering the Blue Economy & Forest Restoration for a Sustainable Future

Keishan is committed to fostering sustainable ocean-based economic growth while preserving marine ecosystems. In addition to our marine initiatives, we actively support forest restoration projects in Kenya, aimed at combating deforestation and promoting biodiversity.

Our solutions drive innovation in marine conservation, aquaculture, renewable energy, and eco-friendly maritime practices, alongside reforestation and sustainable land-use management in Kenya.

Our core principles:

  • Dignity:Change to: Promote sustainable conservation practices that respect both people and the environment .
  • People and planet: Focus on restoring terrestrial and marine ecosystems while supporting local communities
  • Cross Cultural Collaboration: Work with local communities to ensure sustainable use and conservation of marine and terrestrial ecosystems
  • Transparency and Accountability: Ensure responsible use of conservation resources for the benefit of people and the planet.
  • Strategic: Develop sustainable development frameworks to support conservation of threatened ecosystems
  • Hands-on: Actively engage in marine conservation efforts, keeping the focus on ocean health, not personal gain.

KEISHAN is a company incorporated and working in Kenya to restore degraded lands through the living Farms agroforestry model. The living farm is a simple co-created concept of agroecology, a nature protection process that is building a global community of people that care deeply about the planet and want to protect and restore nature.

Join us in creating solutions that balance economic growth with environmental stewardship, both in marine ecosystems and on land.

Advancing Sustainable Marine and Forest Ecosystem Practices

Our projects focus on ensuring the sustainable management and protection of both marine and terrestrial ecosystems, promoting long-term biodiversity. In Kenya, we support initiatives aimed at forest restoration alongside our marine ecosystem preservation efforts.

  • Focused on reducing human impact on marine and terrestrial life.
  • Restoration efforts to revitalize coastal, oceanic, and forest environments, including initiatives in Kenya.
  • Promoting ecosystem-based management of marine and forest resources.

By fostering a circular economy model and supporting eco-friendly marine technologies, we aim to balance human needs with environmental sustainability both at sea and on land.

Embracing Sustainable Innovation: Advancing the Blue and Green Economy

Our mission is to drive sustainable practices in ocean industries and forest restoration efforts while balancing economic growth with environmental protection. In Kenya, we are actively supporting forest restoration alongside our marine conservation activities.

  • Empowering sustainable growth in both marine and forest industries.
  • Supporting ocean conservation and forest regeneration through innovative solutions.
  • Promoting circular economy principles for both marine and forest resource use.

Harnessing the Power of Ocean and Forest Resources Responsibly
